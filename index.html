When my now wife and I were dating, like any couple in a relatively serious relationship, had planned weekly dates. Both of us were very busy and this was a great way for us to take some time for each other. I, the great boyfriend that I was (my wife will certainly have a differing opinion), took the initiative to plan our dates. My best intentions got the better of me most of the time, and was always struggling to pull the activities of the date together. Even something like a simple movie night in required me to get food ordered, flowers purchased, and movie rented from 3 different places. All I wanted was one place to go for dating so that someone else could do all the hardwork , and I got to reap the benefits of the ever-so-thoughtful boyfriend.

Date2Go is a service that provides busy..I mean lazy...I mean busy...people with packaged dates. The planning, the ordering, the scheduling is all done for you. The only thing you have to now worry about is what you are going to say during the date. Date2Go is an intelligent dating service where people are able to log in and select their, as well as their significant other's interests, and location among other biographic information (name, age, etc.). You get to either select recurring or one-time dates. Date2Go then sends you packaged dates, and does all the planning for you (makes dinner reservations, orders movie tickets, orders concert tickets, etc.), so that you can focus on your date rather than all the planning.

What are the pricing tiers for Date2Go?
Pricing will be based either for a single date package, or through a subscription fee for ongoing and recurring date packages.
Will the Date Packages be shipped?
It depends on the date. For example - For concert tickets, tickets will be left at the venue will call (if applicable). For dinner reservations, reservations will be made. For flowers, they will be sent to the indicated shipping address.
What if my significant other is interested only in certain things or is allergic to a certain kind of food?
Users will have the ability to select from a wide variety of options and criteria so that dates can be catered and filtered according to you and your significant other's needs and desires. If desired, the designated date planner, can confirm final approval to make sure that all criteria are appropriately handled.
